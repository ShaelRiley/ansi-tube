## Single purpose

Transform user-selected YouTube playback into configurable ANSI-inspired visual art with closely related optional playback effects.

## Detailed description

Turn ordinary YouTube videos and Shorts into live ANSI-inspired art—entirely inside your browser.

ANSI Tube samples the active video's decoded frames locally and redraws them with optimized ANSI, ASCII, a homebrew 64-shape video alphabet, mosaic blocks, Braille, emoji, or multitone vector lines.

Main features:

- Dozens of standard, monochrome, retro-hardware, and creative palettes
- Color depths from 2 colors through True Color
- Crop, zoom, or squash into 4:3 and 1:1 frames
- Optional low-cost phosphor trails, particles, auras, rays, and outlines
- Optional tape, bit-crush, pitch, radio, and underwater audio treatments
- PNG and ANSI frame export
- Keyboard-accessible controls, reduced-motion support, and persistent compact mode
- MooBurst🐄 unlocks recurring blond-cow apparitions that can roam across every palette, with an adjustable locally synthesized moo and sound-test button

Privacy is simple: there are no accounts, ads, analytics, trackers, external servers, or remote code. Video and audio processing stays on the device. ANSI Tube runs only on YouTube and stores only the user's preferences locally.

ANSI Tube is an independent project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by YouTube or Google.
